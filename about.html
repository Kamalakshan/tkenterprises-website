T.K. Enterprises is a professional, independent fire protection solutions provider established in 1991. We have over 20 years experience protecting industries, private homes, business premises and livelihoods from the devastating effects of fire. We are dedicated to Industrial Safety & personal Protective equipments.

We have a very wide product range covering right from Head protection up to Feet protection, Respiratory Protection, Hand & Arm Protection, Fall Protection, Eye Protection, Hearing Protection, Electrical Safety Products, Body Protection, & Lock Out Tag Out (LOTO) equipments.

We pride ourselves on providing the very latest in fire protection equipment and training seminars at extremely competitive prices.

We believe in providing a personal and proactive service, combined with the highest possible regard for your personal safety, and that of your family and livelihood.
